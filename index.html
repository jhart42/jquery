Savants of Soul: A Gainesville Treasure

By Jacqueline Hart

In this project, I learned how to toggle images to text during the mouseover event. All photography featured on the page is my original work. These photos were taken while I was representing Swamp Records, a student-run record label company. The concert was at a downtown Gainesville bar called the High Dive.
